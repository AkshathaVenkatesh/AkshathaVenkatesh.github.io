About Us

  • Clothing

    Our offering of women's clothing is updated daily with the latest must-have pieces you need to be wearing this season. From celebrity inspired women's fashion to the latest in trend lead styles, keep your look current and shop everything from dresses for any occasion, cute co-ords, tailored duster jackets, fine knits and cut out jumpsuits. Build up your new season wardrobe and opt for transitional pieces in effortlessly cool denim, luxe sheer detailing, muted colour hues and chic tailoring. Make us your go-to destination to ensure your style is looking on-point for any occasion.

  • Shoes

    Put your best foot forward and step out in style this season with our collection of celebrity inspired and on-trend women's shoes to suit any occasion. Keep your feet sweet in everything from killer heels, chic courts, cut out styles, effortlessly cool pumps to elegant pointed toe designs. From faux suede shoes to reptile prints and eye popping colour hues. Add some height to your daytime denim look, dress up your party pieces or grab a pair of must have boots to top off those minimalist vibes. This season keep it simple in barely there strappy sandals, lace up detailing and cut out designs. Channel this season’s 70’s vibes with suede for days and treat your feet!

  • Bags & Handbags

    Add the finishing touch to any look with our collection of bags and purses. Go casual with an oversized shopper, perfect for daytime strolls, or add the chic in a practical but effortlessly cool backpack. We’ve got the perfect piece for the party girl on the go who needs hands free with our selection of bumbags, or for those glam nights out when you need a little added glitter with our statement clutches and shoulder bags! Go for this seasons favourite suede bag with added tassels or mix in a little texture with a faux fur number. From floral prints, mock croc styles and all out embellishment we’ve got the ultimate arm candy for you this season.
